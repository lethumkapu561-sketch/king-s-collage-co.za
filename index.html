About King's College

King's College is a well-established school situated in the heart of East London, KuGompo City. We offer quality education from Grade RR (age 4) through to Grade 9, providing learners with a strong academic foundation alongside character development, sport, arts, and cultural activities. Our dedicated staff are committed to unlocking every learner's potential in a caring and structured environment.

With EMIS number 200201078, King's College is a registered and recognised institution. Our motto — Sapientia et Scientia (Wisdom and Knowledge) — reflects our belief that true education combines wisdom with knowledge to produce well-rounded, capable individuals.

Admissions

King's College is now open for registrations for 2027. Spaces are available from Grade RR to Grade 9. To apply, please contact us directly or visit the school during office hours. The following documents will be required as part of the application process:

  • Completed application / registration form
  • Certified copy of the learner's birth certificate
  • Certified copy of parent / guardian's ID document
  • Most recent school report (Grade 1 and above)
  • Proof of residential address
  • Immunisation / Road to Health card (Grade RR and R)
